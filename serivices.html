Our services

Crop Insurance

Food security is an important part of our national growth and farmers are the core of this life saving task. Our crop insurance services provide comprehensive coverage to protect farmers' crops against various risks.

We provide the following types of crop insurance:

  • Area yield index insurance
  • Hybrid Cover Insurance
  • Multi-peril cover Insurance
  • Weather index Insurance

The policy covers crops against a combination of perils namely drought, excess rains, flooding, hail damage, frost damage, uncontrollable pests and diseases, fire and related perils.

Livestock Insurance

With AgroTrade Kenya Corp.'s livestock insurance, you gain access to a suite of insurance packages tailored to meet your specific needs. From coverage for individual animals to herd-wide protection, our packages offer flexible options to ensure that your livestock are safeguarded against unforeseen events.

Our livestock insurance services aim to protect farmers' livestock investments. We provide the following types of livestock insurance:

  • Fodder Insurance Cover
  • Herd Cover Insurance
  • Index-Based Livestock Insurance
  • Mortality Cover Insurance

The policy compensates against death of insured livestock as a result of various perils namely uncontrollable diseases including epidemics, calving down, accidents, emergency slaughter on medical grounds, fire and related perils.
